Deepavali

Known as the Festival of Lights Deepavali celebrates the triumph of good over evil and banishes the shadows and spirits of the world with Deepavali lamps. In Sri Lanka, Deepavali is celebrated in October with lavish meals, the exchanging of gifts, abundant fireworks and vibrant lights and lamps galore. Hindu temples organise numerous poja's and celebrations to commemorate this significant event.
