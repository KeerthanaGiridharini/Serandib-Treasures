Sinhala & Tamil New Year

The Sinhala & Tamil New Year is celebrated in April each year and the Sinhalese and Tamil nationals place great importance in the observation of rituals and traditions during this New Year. Auspicious times are allocated for formal procedures such as the bathing, lighting of the hearth, anointing of the oil and partaking in a feast of traditional Sri Lankan treats. New clothes and gifts are exchanged and friends and family members rejoice in the celebrations by participating in game contests held in lieu of the New Year. Much fun is had and the celebration ends with fireworks and quality time spent with loved ones.
