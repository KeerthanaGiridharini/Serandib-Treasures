Navam Perahera

The Navam Perahera takes place on 2 nights during the full moon Navam Poya in February. Organised by Gangaramaya Temple, this outstanding parade takes place in Hunupitiya in Colombo against the beautiful backdrop of Beira Lake. Elephants in illuminated costumes, energetic folk dancers, acrobats, drummers and fire dancers capture your full attention as they parade along the streets of Colombo. Tickets can be bought in advance from the Gangaramaya.
