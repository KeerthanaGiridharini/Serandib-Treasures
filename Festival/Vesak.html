Vesak

Vesak commemorates the three significant events that took place in Lord Buddha's life, namely his birth, enlightement and demise. Vesak is celebrated in a grand way, each May in Sri Lanka and the streets are endowed with vibrant lights, elaborate lanterns and colourful pandols depiciting the many lives of Lord Buddha. A stroll amoung Colombo during Vesak is a magical and enriching experience. The ideal of Vesak is to bring happiness to all and stalls offering free food and beverages line the roads. Buddhist temples organise religious discussions, activities and volunteer sessions and devotees flock to places of volunteer sessions and devotees flock to places of worships dressed in serene white attire.
